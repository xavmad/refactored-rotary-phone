7:37 is a design studio based between Paris and Pula, working at the intersection of architecture, art, culture, design, and fashion.

Our practice balances the digital and physical with a sharp mix of speculation and function—one foot in the future, the other firmly on the ground.

From spaces to objects, fleeting moments to lasting forms, our aim is to invite interaction, shaping how we move, gather, and experience the world around us.

Project: Open House

Year: 2024

Client: Pitch Music & Arts Festival

Open House for Pitch Music and Arts Festival 2024 was a house without a door. Visitors broke in through the windows to access the festival’s communal living room for a slice of home.
